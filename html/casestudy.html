The Problem

How to effectively communicate Sungage Financial's tailored financing options and educational resources to homeowners and installers in order to overcome the challenges associated with financing residential solar projects, such as high upfront costs, limited financing options, complex application processes, lack of awareness, and slow payment processes?

Our Solution

The Animated Video helped to convey Sungage Financial’s message in an engaging and easily understand able format. Additionally, the video emphasized the company’s commitment to diversity and inclusivity, portraying the Sungage team as caring and collaborative partners for solar installers and homeowners. Overall, the animated video played a key role in helping Sungage Financial to achieve their goal of educating their customers about their service and values.

Our Process Brief

The Creabiz team used a comprehensive process to create an animated video for Sungage Financial. We started by gathering information and creating a video brief that outlined the video's objectives and target audience. Next, we developed a script that effectively communicated the key points of the video and refined it through collaboration with the client. After finalizing the script, we worked on creating visual style options and storyboarding the video. We provided voice-over auditions for the client to choose from and then animated the storyboard using 2D and 3D animation techniques. Throughout the process, we maintained open communication with the client to ensure the final product met their needs and preferences. The resulting video was a compelling and informative representation of the client's brand and values.
